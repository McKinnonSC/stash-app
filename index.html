Stash is a program used for recording and storing live TV and then sending it into the cloud with Google Drive.

Developed by a team of school students and supervised by their IT technicians, they are developing a program which will make collecting educational video content much less stressful than it can be currently with other similar systems, and this is because of one key feature, cloud computing.
